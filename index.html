Wei Lee

Wei Lee is a Taiwanese artist, designer and musician based in London. In 2016, He co-founded the band Amazing Show and played as a drummer, while starting his graphic design career by creating cover arts and concert posters for the indie music industry in Taiwan. His works have been included in Asian Typography by Sandu Publishing, and Hanzi•Kanji•Hanja 2 by Viction:ary. From 2019 to 2022, Wei was working at JL Design, one of the leading creative agencies in Taiwan, focusing on brand identity in the field of culture, finance, civic and public. Currently, Wei is studying MA in Computational Arts at Goldsmiths, University of London, Exploring his multidisciplinary practice in design, music and arts.
